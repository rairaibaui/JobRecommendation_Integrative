## System Overview
The Job Recommendation System connects employers with job seekers using advanced AI to match candidates to job postings. It provides a unified interface for posting jobs, applying, and managing applications.

---

## Getting Started
1. Open the website in your browser.
2. Choose your role: Employer or Job Seeker.
3. Register for an account using your email address.
4. Verify your email (if required).
5. Log in to access your dashboard.

---

## For Employers
### Registering an Employer Account
1. Click on "Register as Employer".
2. Fill in your company details and contact information.
3. Submit the registration form.
4. Wait for admin approval (if required).

### Posting a Job
1. Log in to your employer dashboard.
2. Click "Post a Job".
3. Enter job details (title, description, requirements, etc.).
4. Submit the job posting.

### Managing Applicants
1. Go to "My Job Posts".
2. Click on a job to view applicants.
3. Review applicant profiles and resumes.

### Approving Applicants
1. Select an applicant.
2. Click "Approve" or "Reject".
3. Approved applicants will be notified.

---

## For Job Seekers
### Registering a Job Seeker Account
1. Click on "Register as Job Seeker".
2. Fill in your personal details and upload your resume.
3. Submit the registration form.

### Searching for Jobs
1. Log in to your job seeker dashboard.
2. Use the search bar to find jobs by keyword, location, or category.

### Applying for Jobs
1. Click on a job listing.
2. Review the job details.
3. Click "Apply" and confirm your application.

### Tracking Application Status
1. Go to "My Applications".
2. View the status of each application (Pending, Approved, Rejected).
